Frequently Asked Questions

How to book a lesson?
To book a lesson, press on the book now button and view available times on the scheduling page.
Do I need a valid license?
Yes, a valid G2 or G license is required.
Are the lessons beginner friendly?
Absolutely. We assess your level before the lesson, and pick up from there!
Are these lessons eligible for a MTO Certification?
No, these lessons only teach fully licensed individuals how to operate a manual transmission. A valid G2 or a G license is required to take these lessons.
How do you process payments?
Payments are handled in-person at the start of the lesson. We take either e-transfer or cash.
How do I book the 3-Lesson package?
When you click on "Book Now", the scheduling page appears. Pick a date and a time, and press "Select and choose another time" and add another lesson.
What is the cancellation/rescheduling policy?
For cancellations and rescheduling, please allow for a 24 hour notice. You can cancel/reschedule through the booking appointment sent to your email.

Where Can You Find Us?

Driving lessons are usually conducted in Mississauga, specifically in the Streetsville Go Station parking lot. The address is:

45 Thomas St, Mississauga, ON L5M 1Y7
